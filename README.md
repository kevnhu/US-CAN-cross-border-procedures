# Flying to the US

## Canada to US

### 1. Get a CBP Sticker

- Apply/verify at: <https://dtops.cbp.dhs.gov/main/#conveyance-aircraft>
- Confirm sticker is valid
- A receipt will suffice if the sticker has not been received in the mail yet

### 2. Choose Port of Entry

- Choose a CBP location near the border (will have "International" in the airport name)
- Confirm fuel/FBO services are available

### 3. Pre-Call Port of Entry to Confirm CBP Availability

Call the customs office at the airport. Preferably more than 24 hrs prior.

| Port of Entry | Phone/Info |
|---|---|
| Ogdensburg | <https://www.cbp.gov/about/contact/ports/ogdensburg-new-york-0701> |
| Massena | <https://www.cbp.gov/about/contact/ports/massena-new-york-0704> |
| Watertown | <https://www.cbp.gov/about/contact/ports/alexandria-bay-new-york-0708> |
| Burlington | <https://www.cbp.gov/about/contact/ports/burlington-international-airport-vermont-0207> |

- Record the customs agent's name

### 4. Check Weather

- Check weather 24 hrs prior
- Decide ETA +/- 15 mins

### 5. File eAPIS (Advanced Passenger Information System)

- File at: <https://eapis.cbp.dhs.gov/eapis/auth>
- Preferably file more than 24 hrs prior
- Record the eAPIS confirmation number and confirmation email

### 6. Call CBP to Confirm Landing Rights

- Call customs office as soon as eAPIS is filed
- Call airport (phone number in A/FD) between 2 and 24 hours before arrival
- Must be at least 1 hour ahead of arrival

### 7. File Flight Plan (1-866-WX-BRIEF)

- Make sure to activate the flight plan
- Obtain a squawk code

### 8. Fly

- Verify all documents are on board
- **Must** be with flight following / talking to a controller when crossing the border
  - Boston Centre or Montreal Centre if going to Ogdensburg or Massena

### 9. Taxi to Customs Office

- **DO NOT** leave the plane
- Wait for the customs official to come to you

## US to Canada (CANPASS)

Reference: <https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca/prog/canpass/privateair-eng.html>

### 1. Plan and Choose a Port of Entry

- Must be a CANPASS-designated port (AOE/15/30...)

### 2. Check Weather

- Check weather 24 hrs prior
- Decide ETA +/- 15 mins

### 3. Call CBSA / CANPASS

- **Phone:** 1-888-226-7277 (1-866-CAN-PASS)
- Minimum +3 hrs notice before landing
- Provide arrival time and location
- This is the phone number listed in the CFS under the CUST section
- Record the **report number** (confirmation number)
- Telephone reporting info: <https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca/prog/canpass/generalavi-eng.html>

### 4. File eAPIS Departure

- File at least 1 hour prior
- <https://eapis.cbp.dhs.gov/eapis/auth>

### 5. File Flight Plan

- Make sure to activate the flight plan and obtain a squawk code
- How do you file on the US side? *(to be confirmed)*
- **Must** be an activated flight plan -- call to double check, otherwise it will result in a CADOR

### 6. Fly

- Pick up flight following across the border

### 7. Call CANPASS Again on Arrival

- Clearance will likely be given over the phone (no one waiting at the airport)
- Reference: <https://www.cbsa-asfc.gc.ca/prog/canpass/generalavi-eng.html>
